# Getting Started with Gospel Metacasts

Welcome! This guide will help you start listening to General Conference talks and other faith-promoting podcasts on your phone.

## What Is Gospel Metacasts?

Gospel Metacasts lets you subscribe to podcasts containing talks from General Conference, Sunday School lessons, and more. You can listen using any podcast app you already have on your phone.

## iPhone Users

### What You'll Need

Your iPhone already has a podcast app built in called **Apple Podcasts**. We'll use that.

### How to Subscribe

1. **Open Safari** (the web browser with the compass icon)
2. **Go to the website**: Visit [SITE_URL]
3. **Browse or Search**:
   - Tap **Browse Feeds** to see all available podcasts
   - Or tap **Search Episodes** to find specific talks
4. **Choose a Podcast**: Tap on the podcast you want
5. **Copy the Feed URL**:
   - Tap the **Copy Feed** button
   - The feed URL will be copied to your clipboard
6. **Add to Podcast App**:
   - Open **Apple Podcasts** app
   - Tap **Library** → tap the **...** (three dots) icon → tap **Follow a Show by URL**
   - Paste the URL you copied and tap **Follow**

### How to Listen

1. Open the **Apple Podcasts** app on your phone
2. Tap **Library** at the bottom
3. Tap your new podcast under "Shows"
4. Tap an episode to play it

**Tip:** You can download episodes to listen offline (like in the car or on a plane). Just tap the download icon next to an episode.

## Android Users

### What You'll Need

You can use any podcast app. Popular free ones include:
- **Google Podcasts** (download from Play Store)
- **Spotify** (download from Play Store)
- **Pocket Casts** (download from Play Store)

### How to Subscribe (Using Google Podcasts as Example)

1. **Open Chrome** (the web browser with the colorful circle)
2. **Go to the website**: Visit [SITE_URL]
3. **Browse or Search**:
   - Tap **Browse Feeds** to see all available podcasts
   - Or tap **Search Episodes** to find specific talks
4. **Choose a Podcast**: Tap on the podcast you want
5. **Copy the Feed URL**:
   - Tap the **Copy Feed** button
   - The feed URL will be copied to your clipboard
6. **Add to Podcast App**:
   - Open **Google Podcasts** (or your chosen app)
   - Tap **Library** → tap **+** → tap **Add by URL**
   - Paste the URL you copied and tap **Subscribe**

### How to Listen

1. Open **Google Podcasts** (or your chosen app)
2. Tap **Home** at the bottom
3. Find your podcast under "Recent Updates" or tap **Library**
4. Tap an episode to play it

**Tip:** You can download episodes to listen offline. In Google Podcasts, tap the download icon next to any episode.

## Need Help?

- **Can't find the podcast app?** Search "Podcasts" in your app store (App Store for iPhone, Play Store for Android) and download a podcast app.
- **Questions about a specific episode?** Every episode includes the speaker's name and topic tags to help you find talks you want.
- **Want to listen to a specific speaker?** Use the **Search Episodes** feature and type the speaker's name (like "Russell Nelson" or "Gordon Hinckley").
